Welcome to nafoai.com ("the Website"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information. By accessing or using the Website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

- Token Information: We collect and store information related to the tokens you receive in exchange for your donation. This includes the number of tokens, and their usage history.
- Generated Images: We store images generated using the Website. These images can be made publicly available upon your consent.
- Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the Website, including pages visited, time spent on each page, and referring website addresses.

- Analytics: We may use usage information to analyze and improve the Website's performance and user experience.

## Disclosure of Information
